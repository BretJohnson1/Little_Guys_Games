Little Guys Games
Let Me Explain...
Founded in 2025, Little Guys Games is an independent game studio based in Tulsa, OK, with a mission to support and empower new game developers. Born out of the challenges faced by our founder during their own journey into game development, the studio was created as a space where aspiring developers can learn, grow, and build a portfolio while collaborating with a team of passionate peers. At Little Guys Games, we believe that everyone deserves a chance to pursue their dreams, regardless of their background or level of experience. We provide opportunities for driven individuals to gain hands-on experience, overcome barriers to entry in the industry, and prepare for their next steps—whether that’s joining a AAA studio or continuing to create innovative games with us. Our team understands what it’s like to start small but dream big. Thanks to faith, family, and friends, we’ve turned this vision into reality, and we’re excited to help others do the same. Little Guys Games is made by the little guys, for the little guys, because that's who we are, and that's who we care about.

Little Guys Games is not a big studio, we need your help and support!
